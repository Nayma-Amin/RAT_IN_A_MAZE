🐀 How to Play — Rat’s Great Escape!

  • 🎲 Generate a new maze — every time is a new adventure!
  • 💡 Click the Hint to get hint! The hint animates only once. FOCUS!
  • ⏱ Click Start to begin a countdown. The rat must escape before time runs out!
  • 🧭 Click cells next to the rat to move — but beware! Walls (red blocks) will trap you!
  • 🎯 Reach the golden exit before time’s up to win!
  • 💡 After you reach the exit, click Solve to see the perfect escape path.
  • 🏆 Find the way, watch the animation, and celebrate your success!
